📂 Facebook Group Collections

Create and manage collections of Facebook groups for targeted posting

💡 How to Use Group Collections

Group collections allow you to organize Facebook groups and post to specific sets of groups.

  • Create collections for different posting strategies (e.g., "Tech Groups", "Marketing Groups")
  • Add Facebook groups to collections from your saved group URLs
  • Post to specific collections instead of all groups at once
  • Edit collections anytime to add or remove groups
